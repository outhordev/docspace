## Character Design Philosophy

All characters should feel like they **belong to the ocean**. Even landlocked characters wear weathered clothing with nautical influences.

### Silhouette Rules

1. **Tideborn** characters are tall and lean, with flowing garments
2. **Ironclad** characters are broad and angular, with hard edges
3. **Echokeeper** characters are obscured — hoods, masks, layered robes

### Faction Color Coding

Each faction has a primary and accent color visible in their clothing:

- **Tideborn Compact**: Deep teal (#0D9488) with coral accent (#FB923C)
- **Ironclad Assembly**: Gunmetal grey (#6B7280) with brass (#D97706)
- **Echokeepers**: Deep purple (#7C3AED) with ghostly blue (#93C5FD)

### Scale Reference

| Character Type | Height (units) | Width | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Human (standard) | 1.8 | 0.5 | Base reference |
| Ironclad Golem | 3.2 | 1.8 | Player-controlled construct |
| Echo Spirit | 1.2 | 0.8 | Translucent, floats |

> [!TIP]
> Always design characters at 2x the in-game resolution, then downsample. This ensures pixel art clarity at all zoom levels.
