## Major Factions

### The Tideborn Compact

A loose alliance of sea-faring settlements. They control most trade routes and believe the ocean itself is sentient.

**Leader:** Admiral Kessa Vane
**Base:** Drowned Market
**Philosophy:** "The sea provides; the sea demands."

### The Ironclad Assembly

Engineers and builders who salvage Elder Council technology. They distrust magic and rely on mechanical constructs.

**Leader:** Forgemaster Dren Kallis
**Base:** The Iron Reach
**Philosophy:** "What is built endures. What is conjured fades."

> [!WARNING]
> The Ironclad Assembly views all magic users with suspicion. Player characters with arcane abilities will face social penalties in Assembly territories.

### The Echokeepers

Scholars and mystics who study the Undercurrent. They believe the Cataclysm can be reversed.

**Leader:** The Listener (identity unknown)
**Base:** The Whispering Depths
**Philosophy:** "Memory is the fifth element."

## Faction Relations

- **Tideborn Compact** ↔ **Ironclad Assembly** — Trade Alliance
- **Tideborn Compact** ↔ **Echokeepers** — Uneasy Truce
- **Ironclad Assembly** → **Echokeepers** — Open Hostility

## Minor Factions

- **The Driftwood Syndicate** — smugglers and pirates
- **The Coral Priesthood** — religious order devoted to the sea
- **The Cartographers' Guild** — mapmakers who charge for knowledge
