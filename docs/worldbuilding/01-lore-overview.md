## The Sunken World

The world of **Meridia** was once a single vast continent, ruled by the Elder Councils. When the Cataclysm struck in the Third Age, the land fractured into a sprawling archipelago.

### The Three Ages

1. **Age of Stone** — The primordial era before recorded history
2. **Age of Towers** — Rise of the Elder Councils and their arcane constructs
3. **Age of Tides** — Post-Cataclysm, the current era of exploration

> [!NOTE]
> The exact cause of the Cataclysm is disputed among scholars. Some believe it was a weapon; others say it was an accident.

## Cosmology

The world operates on a **tripartite cosmological model**:

- **The Oversky** — realm of celestial beings
- **The Midlands** — the mortal plane
- **The Undercurrent** — a shadow-realm accessible through deep water

The three planes are linked in a cycle: The Oversky feeds divine energy down to the Midlands, which drains into the Undercurrent, whose echoes whisper back up to the Oversky.
