## World Timeline

### Age of Stone (??? – Year 0)

- **Unknown** — First life emerges from the Undercurrent
- **Unknown** — The Old Gods shape the continent of Meridia
- **Year 0** — The Elder Councils establish the first written calendar

### Age of Towers (Year 0 – Year 2,847)

- **Y 120** — Construction of the Obsidian Spire begins
- **Y 450** — The Seven Wards are established, dividing Meridia into governed regions
- **Y 1,200** — First contact with the Undercurrent documented
- **Y 2,300** — The Echokeeper Order is founded
- **Y 2,847** — **The Cataclysm** — Meridia shatters

### Age of Tides (Year 2,848 – Present)

- **Y 2,848** — Survivors establish the first island settlements
- **Y 2,900** — The Tideborn Compact is formed
- **Y 3,050** — The Ironclad Assembly recovers the first Elder Engine
- **Y 3,120** — The Drowned Market becomes the de facto capital
- **Y 3,200** — **Present day** — tensions rise between factions

> [!TIP]
> When writing narrative content, refer to dates as "Y 3,200" format. The calendar has no month/day system — only yearly cycles and tidal phases.
